Brickwall Community - DarkRP Rules

General

  • Blocking or exploiting the Gun Dealer NPC is strictly prohibited.

Roleplay Guidelines

  • Use common sense at all times.

Definitions

  • RDM (Random Deathmatch): Killing without a valid in-character reason. Will be punished harshly.
  • FailRP: Doing something unrealistic for your role. E.g., pulling a gun while being mugged at gunpoint.
  • NLR (New Life Rule): You forget everything after death. No revenge. Do not return during raids. NLR lasts 3 minutes.
  • KOS (Kill on Sight): KOS signs are prohibited. You must have a valid reason to kill someone.
  • Metagaming: Don’t use OOC or external info in RP. Think like your in-game character.

Raiding

  • Do not advertise your raid — that’s unrealistic.
  • You must wait 15 minutes before raiding the same base again.

Mugging

  • Advertising a mug is not allowed.
  • The maximum amount you can mug for is $2,000.
  • You may not mug players who clearly have no money (e.g., Hobos).
  • Mugging someone who is holding a weapon is considered FailRP.

Job-Specific Rules

Gun Dealer

  • You may buy a weapon for self-defense.
  • Self-supplying and switching jobs immediately after is considered FailRP.

Mayor

  • Laws must be reasonable. Laws like Killing is allowed are forbidden.
  • You may build anywhere as long as you don’t prop block or obstruct NPCs.

Civil Protection

  • Lethal force is only allowed when your life is in danger, and only after attempting to arrest.
  • You need a valid reason to raid (e.g., gunfire, explosions).

Base Building

  • One-way props are not allowed.
  • Maximum of 2 fading doors between entrance and valuables.
  • Each fading door must allow 2 players to fit between them.
  • Keypads must remain unlocked for at least 15 seconds after being cracked.

Expression 2 (E2) and Wiremod

Expression 2

  • Abusing E2 for an advantage (e.g., printer finders, wallhacks) is strictly forbidden.
  • E2 door abuse (spamming doors to gain an edge) is not allowed.

Wiremod / StarfallEx

  • Any abuse of Wiremod or StarfallEx will result in severe punishment.
